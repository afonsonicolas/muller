ABOUT Müller JG

Joyeria Muller JG is the culmination of over 100 years of family experience in luxury jewelry, offering high-quality Italian, Japanese, and American design.


As the exclusive retailers of Roberto Coin, Marco Bicego, Mikimoto, Piero Milano, Doves, and Bridal JG, our jewelers carefully handpick a variety of precious and semiprecious pieces ranging from trendsetting to classic fashion across multiple price points.


We take pride in offering unparalleled service, and we will dedicate as much time and expertise as you need to find the jewelry piece of you or your loved one’s dreams. Visit us at our locations in Avenida Escazu, Multiplaza Curridabat, or our newest location in Aleste.
